## Chapter Distribution Priority

When filling Phase 1:
1. **Weakest subjects first**: front-load their ✮✮✮✮ chapters in weeks 1–2
2. **Weakest subject second pass**: if time, schedule a review run through high-yield chapters in the back half of Phase 1
3. **Mixed-strength subjects**: schedule in the middle of Phase 1
4. **Strongest subjects**: schedule in the final third of Phase 1 before AAMC phase
5. **Low-yield chapters (✮) in strong subjects**: can be covered quickly or skipped if schedule is tight
6. **High-yield chapters (✮✮✮✮) in weak subjects**: always covered; schedule earlier and consider repeating
